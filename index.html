What is SmartTV Twitch?

SmartTV Twitch is a web application designed to bring the full Twitch experience to Smart TVs. Since the official Twitch app isn't available on most TV platforms, this project fills that gap.

The app is split into two parts: a web app that handles the user interface and most of the logic, and an Android APK that wraps it for Android TV devices with native player support, notifications, and hardware integration.

It uses the official Twitch API to access all content — this is not a screen scraper or unofficial hack. It's a properly registered application that works within Twitch's developer guidelines.

The project is completely free, has no ads, and is open source under the GPL-3.0 license.

What's Included

A full-featured Twitch experience designed from the ground up for TV remotes and big screens.

Live Streams, VODs & Clips

Watch any live channel, browse past broadcasts, highlights, and clips. Supports all stream qualities from source to mobile.

Multi-Stream Viewing

Watch multiple streams at the same time on a single screen. Perfect for following tournaments or keeping tabs on your favorites.

Full Twitch Chat

Real-time chat with full emote support (including Twemoji), badges, and the ability to send messages. Sub-only and gifted sub notifications included.

Twitch Account Login

Sign in with your Twitch account to see followed channels, check subscriptions, access personalized recommendations, and chat as yourself.

Browse Games & Categories

Explore Twitch by game, category, or search. See top streams, trending games, and discover new content to watch.

Customizable Settings

Adjust stream quality, chat appearance, playback speed, notification preferences, virtual D-pad position, and more. Make the app work the way you want.

Privacy Focused

No ads, no tracking, no data collection. The app only communicates with Twitch's official API. Your data stays between you and Twitch.

Multi-Language Support

Translated into multiple languages by the community. Easy translation system so anyone can contribute their language.

Open Source

100% open source under GPL-3.0. Inspect the code, fork it, contribute, or build your own version. Community contributions are welcome.

Credits & Origins

This project wouldn't exist without the work of the original developers.

Original Project

SmartTV Twitch was originally created by nikitakatchik as a Samsung Tizen OS application for watching Twitch on Smart TVs. It was later forked and improved by CazuzaCzz.

The version you see today is maintained by fgl27, who rebuilt the application from the ground up — focusing on performance, full Twitch API support, and Android TV as the primary platform. Over time it grew from a simple Tizen TV app into a full-featured, multi-platform Twitch client with an active community of users and contributors.

This fork is based on fgl27's work. All credit for the core application goes to these developers and the contributors who have helped shape it over the years.

  • CazuzaCzz/SmartTwitchTV — Earlier fork
